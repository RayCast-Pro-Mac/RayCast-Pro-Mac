## Overview

RayCast Pro Professional Suite is a complete productivity platform that serves teams with shared extension configurations, AI tools, and workflow integrations that keep everyone working from the same efficient, keyboard-driven environment.

Team configurations allow organizations to standardize extension setups, shared snippets, and custom commands across team members, ensuring everyone benefits from the same productivity optimizations without individual setup for each person.

Raycast AI Pro enables longer context AI conversations, AI-powered command creation, and access to multiple AI models from within the launcher, making sophisticated AI assistance available without switching to a separate application.

Floating Notes and Quick Capture tools allow ideas and information to be captured immediately in context without disrupting the current workflow, keeping notes and the work they relate to connected.

RayCast Pro Professional Suite is the productivity platform for teams that want every member operating at peak efficiency, with the AI capabilities and extension ecosystem to support any workflow any team member encounters.

## Performance & Integration

RayCast Pro is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while RayCast Pro processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows

## System Requirements

- macOS 12 Monterey or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel — Universal Binary
- Works with macOS Ventura, Sonoma and Sequoia
